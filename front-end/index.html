How does this work?

Generative Art is a multi-step process:

Fund

Fund the smart contract to commission the art

Randomness

An oracle generates a random number and word

Create

The smart contract translates the number into SVG code

Art

The SVG code and metadata are stored on the blockchain

So what do I need to do?

TLDR: press 'Generate Art', approve 3 MetaMask prompts, and be very patient 1. Connect your MetaMask to this app (Matic Mumbai testnet, for testnet $MATIC and $LINK click here )
2. Press the 'Generate Art' button
3. Approve the MetaMask prompt to fund this contract
4. Wait a few seconds and approve the MetaMask prompt for the create transaction
5. Wait 30 seconds and approve the MetaMask prompt for the finish mint transaction
6. Wait a while longer, maybe get a cup of tea…
7. Press the 'Refresh Gallery' button (the page will reload if your new NFT is ready)
8. Congratulations! You are now the proud owner of a piece of truly random digital art
